On left-right γ̄-ε-condition pseudospectrum Published
I. Ferjani, M. Beghdadi & B. Krichen · Kragujevac Journal of Mathematics, 51(5):887–899, 2027

In this paper, we introduce new pseudo-spectral sets called the left (right) γ-pseudospectrum and left (right) γ-ε-condition pseudospectrum of bounded linear operators on Banach spaces. We provide a sufficient condition for invertibility using the Kuratowski measure of noncompactness, and establish characterizations of these new spectral sets.

Study of relatively generalized weakly demicompact (GWMD) operators on Banach spaces. New characterizations via De Blasi measure of noncompactness, stability under perturbations, analysis of 2×2 block operator matrices, and numerical experiments on weighted-shift and Volterra-type operators.
